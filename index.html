I Love Neutrinos

An annual STEAM course.

About

I love neutrinos (ILN), it is an annual program STEAM (Science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics).

The program aims to bring students to science, enhance the reflective / creative thinking and social skills development through the development of educational projects using the Lego Mindstorms and Raspberry Pi.
